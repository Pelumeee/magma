Who we are

Built for Africa's toughest projects

Magma Technical Services is a trusted provider of engineered lifting, crane rental, heavy transport, and specialised project support solutions for Nigeria’s oil and gas, petrochemical, energy, construction, power, manufacturing, marine, aviation, and infrastructure sectors.

Our capabilities support the full lifecycle of industrial and infrastructure projects, from construction and plant expansion to maintenance shutdowns, drilling operations, equipment installation, commissioning, and emergency response.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s most trusted partner for engineered lifting and specialised transport solutions, recognised for safety, reliability, and operational excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, engineered lifting and transport solutions that help clients execute complex projects with confidence, while creating lasting value through technical expertise, dependable service, and an uncompromising commitment to safety.

HSE Commitment

Safety without compromise.

HSE excellence is the foundation of every operation. Through rigorous planning, risk assessment, continuous training and strict compliance with industry best practices, we protect our people, clients, communities and the environment on every project.

Core services

What we do

Six integrated service lines covering the full heavy-industrial scope, from the first lift plan to the last load delivered to site.

01

ENGINEERED HEAVY LIFT

We plan and execute complex lifting operations using modern equipment and customised methods tailored to our client's project requirements.

02

CRANE HIRE

We provide modern cranes with certified operators, rigging personnel, lift supervision, and complete project management for safe and seamless execution.

03

HEAVY TRANSPORT

We transport oversized and overweight loads, handling the entire process from routing planning to load-out/load-in operations.

04

PLANT SUPPORT

We provide crane and lifting solutions for refinery turnarounds, petrochemical facilities, power plants, drilling operations, equipment replacement, and planned maintenance activities.

05

RIGGING & INSTALLATION

We provide precision lifting, machinery relocation, structural steel erection, modular assembly, equipment installation, skidding, jacking, and positioning of heavy industrial assets.

06

PROJECT ENGINEERING & LIFT PLANNING

We perform engineering studies, lift plans, risk assessments, 3D lift simulations, site inspections, and constructability reviews to ensure safe and efficient project delivery.
