Who we are

Built for Nigeria's toughest projects

Magma Technical Services Nigeria Ltd is an indigenous service firm delivering quality heavy-lift and logistics solutions to the Oil & Gas, Construction and Power sectors.

From planning through to post-completion, we mobilise the right crew, the right equipment and the right engineering to keep critical infrastructure moving — safely, on schedule, and to spec.

Core services

What we do

Six integrated service lines covering the full heavy-industrial scope — from the first lift plan to the last load delivered to site.

01 — Primary

Crane Hire

Heavy-lift crane operations up to 100 tonnes. Link-Belt RTC-80100 rough-terrain and American cranes for industrial, oil & gas and construction sites.

02 — Primary

Marine Logistics

Tugboats, barges, landing craft and offshore support. 24/7 operations across Nigeria's coastal and inland waterways.

03 — Primary

Rigging & Lifting

Specialist rigging, load planning and lifting engineering. Ropes, hawsers, shackles and chains for complex heavy-lift operations.

04

Land Transport

Flatbeds, lowbeds, trailers and crew transport. Nationwide mobilisation for project logistics, on time and on plan.

05

Engineering & EPC

FEED, mechanical, electrical and instrumentation engineering. Civil, structural and full EPC delivery for industrial assets.

06

Procurement

Global supply chain for drilling fluids, chemicals, PPE, spare parts and heavy equipment. OEM-grade sourcing, delivered to site.
