Who we are

From planning to post-completion

Magma Technical Services Nigeria Ltd was founded to close a hard gap: world-class heavy-lift capability, owned and run by Nigerians, for Nigerian conditions.

We bring crane hire, marine logistics, rigging, land transport, engineering and procurement under one accountable roof — so clients in Oil & Gas, Construction and Power don't have to stitch together a dozen contractors on the critical path.

From the first lift study through to demobilisation, our crews plan it, rig it, move it and stand it up — to spec, on schedule, and to a zero-harm standard.

Track record

Years on the iron

A short history of building heavy-lift capability across Nigeria's most demanding sites.

2016

Founded in Port Harcourt

Magma is established as an indigenous heavy-lift and logistics firm, headquartered in the Trans Amadi industrial layout, Rivers State.

2018

Marine spread online

Tugboats, barges and landing craft join the fleet, extending operations across coastal and inland waterways with 24/7 support.

2020

100T lift capacity

The Link-Belt RTC-80100 rough-terrain crane brings 100-tonne lift capability to industrial, oil & gas and construction sites.

2022

EPC & engineering

Service lines expand into FEED, mechanical, electrical, instrumentation and full EPC delivery for industrial assets.

Today

On the critical path

Trusted on refinery rehabilitation, plant maintenance, drilling support and naval logistics across the country.
