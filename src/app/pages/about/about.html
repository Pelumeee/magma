Who we are

From planning to completion

Magma Technical Services Nigeria Ltd is a trusted provider of engineered lifting, crane rental, heavy transport, and specialised project support solutions.

From planning to completion, we help clients execute some of their most challenging projects safely, efficiently, and with certainty by combining experienced personnel, modern equipment and meticulous planning to deliver outcomes that matter.

Our capabilities support the full lifecycle of industrial and infrastructure projects, from construction and plant expansion to maintenance shutdowns, drilling operations, equipment installation, commissioning, and emergency response.

Operating across Nigeria, we serve the oil and gas, petrochemical, energy, manufacturing, marine, aviation, and infrastructure sectors with solutions tailored to each project's unique technical and operational requirements.
